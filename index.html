Academic CV

My name is Sébastien Vandenitte. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Sign Language Centre of the University of Jyväskylä, Finland.

I hold BA (Modern Languages & Literatures) and MA (Linguistics) degrees from Université Libre de Bruxelles and a PhD degree (Languages, Literatures, and Translation Studies) from the LSFB-Lab of the Université de Namur, Belgium.

Research interests

  • (Belgian) French
  • LSFB (French Belgian Sign Language)
  • comparative linguistics and semiotics
  • gesture and multimodality
  • depiction & iconicity
  • the building & use of comparable corpora
  • multi-method approaches to language research
