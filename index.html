About me

My name is Sébastien Vandenitte. I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Sign Language Centre of the University of Jyväskylä (JYU), Finland, working as a member of the ShowTell project.

Background

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Modern Languages and Literatures and a Master’s degree in Linguistics from Université Libre de Bruxelles (ULB). Since completing my dissertation at the LSFB-Lab, I hold a PhD degree in Languages, Literatures, and Translation from Université de Namur (UNamur).

Research interests

My research focuses on:

  • the comparison of signed and spoken languages, in particular LSFB (Langue des Signes de Belgique Francophone or French Belgian Sign Language) and Belgian French
  • semiotic diversity across communities and communication contexts
  • depiction and iconicity
  • the creation and use of multimodal language corpora
